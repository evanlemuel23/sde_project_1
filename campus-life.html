Annual Events & Festivals

🎉 TechFest

3-day technology festival with hackathons, workshops, coding contests, and exhibitions. Attracts participants from across India.

March | 5000+ Participants

🎭 Cultural Fest

Celebration of arts with dance, music, drama, fashion shows, and talent hunt events. Showcase of student creativity and talent.

September | 20+ Events

🏆 Sports Meet

Inter-college sporting extravaganza with multiple disciplines. Builds team spirit and healthy competition among students.

November | 50+ Events

🚀 Innovation Summit

Platform for startup ideas with mentorship from entrepreneurs. Prizes and funding opportunities for best concepts.

May | 200+ Teams

📖 Freshers' Welcome

Orientation and integration program for new students. Icebreakers, talent shows, and campus introduction activities.

August | 1000+ Freshers

🎓 Farewell Ceremony

Emotional sendoff for graduating batch with awards, performances, and celebrations of achievements and memories.

April | Graduating Batch
