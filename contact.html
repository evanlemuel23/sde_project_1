FAQ - Common Questions

How do I apply for admission?

Visit the Admissions page for detailed information about eligibility, application process, and important dates. You can submit your application online through our portal.

What is the placement percentage?

We have a 95% overall placement rate across all departments. CSE has the highest package average of ₹12.5 LPA. Check our Placements page for detailed statistics.

Are scholarships available?

Yes! We offer merit scholarships, need-based scholarships, sports scholarships, and special provisions for SC/ST/OBC and girl students. Contact admissions@cit.edu.in for details.

Is hostel accommodation available?

Yes, we have separate boys' and girls' hostels with modern facilities. Hostel fee is ₹80,000 per annum with meal costs as additional. Priority given to out-station students.

Do you offer M.Tech programs?

Yes! We offer M.Tech programs in CSE, Mechanical, Electrical, and Electronics & Communication. GATE scores are preferred but merit-based admission is also available.

What are the international exchange opportunities?

We have partnerships with 40+ universities globally. Students can participate in semester exchanges, research internships, and collaborative projects. Contact the International Office for details.

Whether you're a prospective student, researcher, or industry partner, we're here to help. Reach out to us today!
