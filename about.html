About Cambridge Institute

25 Years of Excellence in Engineering Education

Our Story

Founded in 2000, Cambridge Institute of Technology emerged from a vision to create an autonomous engineering college that combines rigorous academics with practical innovation. Over two and a half decades, we've evolved into one of India's premier engineering institutions.

Our commitment to academic excellence, research advancement, and student development has made us a beacon of engineering education. With our autonomous status, we have the flexibility to design cutting-edge curricula that keeps pace with industry demands.

About the College:
Cambridge Institute of Technology, K R Puram is a private engineering and technology college located in the Krishnarajapuram (KR Puram) area of Bengaluru, Karnataka. It was established in 2007 and is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU). The institute is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC).
